The trust we give and the trust we take

Mack Fry

Built and burned in a matter of a few words.
This thing we call trust, is something we must earn.
Consistency,
Faithfulness,
Vulnerability,
These things we hope for
when we give the same.

We build brick by brick the
Trust we hope to give and take.
When we extend this precious gem,
We pour in all the grace and loyalty we encompass.
We pay attention, draw near,
We tell secrets,
We lend our ears.

Trust is proven,
as days become years.
Through kindness,
careful consideration,
and shared tears.
We give,
hoping one day we will take the same.
Trust is a prize, the gold medal we play the game to win.

Then comes the hard part,
who?
In whose hands is it safe?
This question voiding answer leads us to curiosity
and eventually a leap of faith.
We must give even
if we never take.
This thing we call trust is something we all have to make.
